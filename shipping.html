Shipping Policy

Last updated: June 2026

How We Ship

All Two Ravens Coffee Co. orders are roasted to order and fulfilled by our roasting partner, Roastify. This means your coffee is roasted fresh before it ships, not pulled from a warehouse shelf.

Processing Time

Most orders are roasted and shipped within 4 to 5 business days of your order being placed. During high-volume periods (holiday promotions, new product launches), processing may take up to 7 business days. You will receive a tracking email as soon as your order ships.

Shipping Rates

Shipping is calculated at checkout based on your location and order size.

  • First bag: $4.50
  • Each additional bag in the same order: $1.25

Delivery Estimates

Once shipped, standard delivery typically takes 3 to 7 business days within the contiguous United States. We currently ship to US addresses only.

Tracking

A tracking number will be emailed to you when your order ships. If you have not received a tracking email within 7 business days of placing your order, please contact us at the email address on your order confirmation.

Delays

Carrier delays are occasionally outside our control. If your package appears stuck in transit for more than 10 business days after shipment, contact us and we will work with you to resolve it.

Questions

Reach out through the contact form on this site. We are a small team and we respond promptly.
