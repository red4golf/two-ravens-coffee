Our Story

Two ravens, and the habit of paying attention.

The name comes from an old Norse idea: two ravens, Thought and Memory, sent out each morning to see what was happening in the world and bring back what mattered. We liked it as a reminder to pay attention to the things that count, and to carry the good ones forward.

For us, that means coffee made carefully and tied to work we believe in. Every bag is roasted to order and shipped fresh, and most of our roasts give back to Angel Wings Transport, which flies animals and people home when they need it.

The Mission Behind the Coffee

What your cup helps make possible.

Angel Wings Transport is a 501(c)(3) network of volunteer pilots across the Northwest: Washington, Oregon, California, Idaho, and Montana. The pilots donate their time and aircraft. What we help support is everything around the flight.

🐾

Animal Transport

Coordinating animals out of overcrowded shelters and into rescues and forever homes, covering crates, health checks, and the logistics that get them there.

✈️

Military & Homecomings

Support for service members and their families, including the long-awaited flight home after a deployment.

➕

Medical Transport

Helping ambulatory patients with a financial need reach the care they're traveling for, through the same regional volunteer network.

Angel Wings Transport operates under FAA regulations; all flights are conducted by volunteer pilots under Part 91. A portion of Two Ravens Coffee Co. sales supports the organization's mission and operational costs as a 501(c)(3) nonprofit. Coffee purchases are not compensation for air transportation.
