Origin

Our
Story

Odin sends Huginn and Muninn out each morning. Thought and Memory. A frame for paying attention to the world. Huginn is Thought: the raven you send out into the uncertain. Muninn is Memory: the one who brings back what matters. Every morning, Odin waits to see if they return. There is something older than most stories about that kind of attention.

AWT Rescue is the real-world version of that myth. Volunteer pilots across the country flying animals in need: dogs, cats, wildlife, creatures that can't speak for what they need. The mission covers operational costs. Transport crates. Vet health checks. Coordination. Not compensation for flights. The work.

Three roasts, three missions. Tailwind for the rescue network. Final Approach for the homecoming flights. 3 AM Blend for the ones who can't sleep because something matters to them.

Roasted to order and shipped within days. The Norse angle isn't a costume. It's a frame. Thought and Memory: what you give your attention to, what you carry forward. Every order helps keep the mission moving.

The Mission Behind the Coffee

AWT Rescue coordinates volunteer aviation for animals and people who need it. Volunteer pilots donate their time and aircraft. The network spans the country. A dog that needs to travel 1,400 miles to reach a rescue gets there because someone with a Cessna said yes.

The same network supports military family homecomings. The moment when someone lands after a long deployment is not a small thing. We help make those flights possible.

Every Two Ravens purchase helps cover operational costs: transport crates, vet health checks, mission coordination, and the infrastructure that keeps the network moving. The flights are flown by volunteers. What you're supporting is everything around the flight that makes it work.

AWT Rescue operates under FAA regulations. All flights are conducted by volunteer pilots under Part 91. Revenue from Two Ravens Coffee Co. supports mission operational costs only and does not constitute compensation for air transportation.
