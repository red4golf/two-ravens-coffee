Return & Refund Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Our Commitment

We stand behind what we sell. Because our products are perishable, roasted to order, and fulfilled fresh, we cannot accept returns of opened or used bags. When something goes wrong, we will make it right.

Damaged or Incorrect Orders

If your order arrives damaged, opened, or if you received the wrong product, contact us within 30 days of delivery with a photo of the item and your order number. We will send a replacement or issue a full refund. Your choice.

Unsealed Packaging

If a bag arrives with a broken seal, damaged packaging, or visible signs of a fulfillment error, please contact us immediately. We will replace the item at no charge.

Not What You Expected

Taste is personal. If you are unhappy with a roast, reach out and tell us. We will do what we can. We do not promise refunds for taste preference, but we will not leave you hanging.

Cancellations

Because orders go to roast quickly, cancellation requests must be submitted within 12 hours of placing your order. After that, your order may already be in production and we may not be able to cancel it.

Refund Processing

Approved refunds are returned to your original payment method within 5 to 10 business days depending on your bank or card issuer.

Contact

Use the contact form on this site or email the address on your order confirmation. Include your order number and a brief description of the issue and we will get back to you within one business day.
